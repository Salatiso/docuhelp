[Family Name] | DNA Policy

1. Purpose

This policy establishes a clear and objective procedure for verifying lineage for the purposes of qualifying as a beneficiary of The [Family Name] Family Trust and/or a shareholder in [Family Business Name]. Its aim is to prevent disputes and ensure fairness for all current and future generations.

2. Policy

In any case where the parentage of an individual claiming to be a descendant of [Founder's Name] is in doubt or formally challenged by a Trustee or Shareholder, DNA testing will be required. The test must establish a direct biological link to the claimed parent, who is a known descendant.

3. Procedure

  • The test must be conducted by a SANAS-accredited laboratory agreed upon by the Trustees/Board.
  • The cost of the testing will be borne by the Trust/Company.
  • The results will be provided directly to the Trustees/Board and will be kept strictly confidential.
  • A positive result confirming the claimed lineage will qualify the individual as a beneficiary/shareholder, subject to all other conditions of the Trust Deed/Shareholders' Agreement. A negative result will disqualify the individual.

4. Agreement & Consent

I, [Full Name of Person Being Tested], hereby consent to undergo DNA testing in accordance with the policy outlined above. I understand that the results will be used to determine my status as a beneficiary/shareholder and that the decision based on these results will be final.
