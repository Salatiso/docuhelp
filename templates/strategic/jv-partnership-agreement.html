1. The Parties

This Joint Venture Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] by and between [Party A Name], with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Party A"), and [Party B Name], with its principal place of business at [Address] ("Party B").

2. Purpose of the Joint Venture

The parties agree to form a joint venture ("JV") for the purpose of [describe the project or business, e.g., 'developing and marketing a new software application for the logistics industry']. The JV will operate under the name [JV Name].

3. Contributions & Ownership

The parties shall contribute to the JV as follows: Party A shall contribute [describe contribution, e.g., 'initial capital of R500,000 and marketing expertise']. Party B shall contribute [describe contribution, e.g., 'all necessary software development and technical support']. Ownership of the JV and any resulting profits or losses shall be split 50/50 between Party A and Party B.

4. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property developed by the JV during the term of this Agreement shall be jointly owned by Party A and Party B in equal 50/50 shares.

5. Term and Termination

This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue until [Date or event], unless terminated earlier by mutual agreement or due to a material breach by either party.
