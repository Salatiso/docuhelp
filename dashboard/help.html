Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new document?

Go to the Templates section, select a template, and start editing. You can save or download your document.

How do I upgrade my account?

From your Profile or Settings, choose "Upgrade" and follow the subscription instructions.

How secure is my data?

All documents are stored securely and encrypted for subscribed users. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Where can I get more help?

Contact support via the Support page or email us directly.
